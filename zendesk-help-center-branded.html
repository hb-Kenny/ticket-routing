Service Level Agreements & Ticket Tiers

Tier Level Support Level Initial Response Resolution
Tier 1 General Support 1 business day 2 business days
Tier 2 Specialized Support 1 business day 3-5 business days
Tier 3 Product Support 1 business day No committed timelines due to the nature of investigations. Periodic updates provided and then the resolution will be provided with the findings.
Tier 4 Engineering Support 1 business day No committed timelines (based on priority)
